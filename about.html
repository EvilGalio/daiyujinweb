No Trespassing

I am a drifting cloud,
churning in skies of reason,
forever pulled by winds unknown.

Logic my steady wings,
inspiration the light cutting through.

Simple as morning dew, clear and transparent,
complex as distant stars, deep and unfathomable.

I walk the boundary of order and chaos,
neither lost nor all-knowing,
just a traveler who keeps asking,
writing an endless poem in a silent journey.

If you're reading this far, you don't need an about page to know me.
